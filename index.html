A fan-made hub dedicated to Morissette’s music, performances, and achievements.

Morissette, hailed as the "Asia’s Phoenix,” continues to rise as one of the most powerful and transcendent voices of her generation. Her unmatched vocal prowess has not only captured the hearts of millions but has also opened doors for her to collaborate with legendary acts such as A1, alongside other respected names in the music industry. She has also shared the stage with some of today’s biggest pop icons and global superstars, proving time and time again that her voice belongs on the world stage.

From the Philippines to the global spotlight, Morissette has consistently carried her country with pride. One of her most iconic moments came when she was chosen to perform the official Asia release of “A Whole New World” for Disney’s live-action Aladdin—a testament to her timeless vocal elegance.

She further solidified her international presence when she represented the Philippines at the Asia Song Festival in Busan, South Korea in 2017 and 2018, making history as the first artist to grace the stage for two consecutive years.

Her journey has taken her across the world, with sold-out concerts in the USA, Dubai, Canada, Australia, Japan, Thailand, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, Italy, and many more—each stage echoing the same truth: Morissette was born to be heard.

Career Timeline

Morissette began her career at a young age, joining TV5’s Talentadong Pinoy at 14 and later competing in Star Factor, finishing as first runner-up. She appeared in TV5 shows and recorded early songs. In 2012, she starred in Disney’s Camp Rock stage musical but experienced vocal strain that led to vocal cord nodules.

In 2013, she joined The Voice of the Philippines and gained national recognition. She reached the semifinals under Sarah Geronimo’s team. In 2014, she became a regular ASAP performer, recorded theme songs, held her first solo concert, and performed with international artist Jessie J.

Morissette rose to major fame, releasing her debut album and earning multiple awards. She became known for hit TV and film theme songs, joined major concerts, and became part of the “Birit Queens.” Her performances went viral globally, earning her the title “Asia’s Phoenix.”

From 2020 onward, Morissette focused on songwriting, production, and international work. She released music such as “Love You Still,” “Phoenix,” and her EP Signature. She performed globally, sang at national events, celebrated her 10th anniversary, and recently expanded into film with Song of the Fireflies (2025), earning Best Actress recognition.
